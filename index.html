About Us

Get to know us

We provide a peer to peer lending solutions that facilitates borrowing and lending money through social interactions, making it affordable to get loans online at a flexible and low interest rate. Our platform also creates a seamless passive income stream for lenders from the interest earn on their loans.

Our mission

To bridge the income inequality gap by creating a decentralized social interaction that enables people meet their financial needs.

Our vision

At NaiScience, our vision is to be the choice peer to peer service in Africa by creating an online community for financial transactions.
