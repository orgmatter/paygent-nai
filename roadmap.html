NAI SCIENCE ROADMAP

Our Solution

Nai Science will offer a peer to peer platform based on decentralized currency, for financial transactions of secured loans.

Product Milestone

Launch NaiScience peer to peer

Launch NaiScience peer to peer securing lending application using cryptocurrency as collateral and offering Naira as loan. The application will match lenders with borrowers at an interest rate within stipulated tenor and structured repayment plan. The application offers lenders about 30% return on investment while offering borrowers flexible repayment plan.

Market Analysis

  1. According to Global Crypto Adoption Index, Nigeria ranks sixth world cryptocurrency adoption.
  2. In 2020, Nigerians traded more that $400m worth of crypto on local crypto exchange platforms.
  3. Nigeria lending percentage increased by 82% according to Nairametric.
  4. Nigerians are fast adopting cryptocurrencies as assets due to decentralization of blockchain technology and ease of acquiring cryptocurrencies in different volumes and price.
  5. The data above positions Nigeria as a very viable market for NaiScience business proposition.
  6. NaiScience projects to attract 100,000 active users in the first year on the lending side, and 10,000 active users on the borrow side, who would use the Naira-denominated funding to speculate in the Solana ecosystem or beyond.

Revenue streams

We project to generate revenue during the first launch in two ways:

  1. Annual loan management charges up to 15% of loan interest per user, we can rehypothecate cheaper sources of funding and have a safe increase stream on a growing balance sheet.
  2. Investing the deposited collateral in BTC Dollarization Carry Trades (Buy BTC spot, sell BTC/USD Futures).
  3. Token appreciation leading to a growing balance sheet without realizing capital gains, we use our own product to source the cheapest Naira funding in the DeFi markets, and gain a net-interest income on that borrow.
  4. The protocol token we will create around our smart contracts on Solana ($NAIS) will accrue a buyback through issuance fees for the origination of new NAI stablecoins, and through liquidations.

What is NAI?

Nai is our stablecoin product named after the Nigerian national currency and DAI, which was the first major secured-loan backed algorithmic stablecoin. We will launch NAI initially as a Tether-like fully-backed stablecoin, but we will not back it with Naira, it will be backed with BTC + USD hedges in offshore exchanges with multi-factor access control preventing anything happening in Nigeria from corrupting, freezing or coercing that money. This is because we are living in history.

As we implement the full scope of Rust-based smart contracts on Solana we will have a NaiScienceDAO that will do everything within the Solana ecosystem, taking SPL BTC or SOL and hedging it with the USD swaps available within SERUM, arbitraging the funding rates in Mango, taking on-chain data of NAI trading on Solana and other chains, and using that to inform the liquidations of NAI debtors’ collateral due to price drops. When there is a liquid options market available, we will integrate that into the DAO and be able to avoid liquidation risk entirely.

The full project of the NAI is simply this: there is no transparent market data for a true market clearing price of the Naira, nor liquidity at that price, and soon there will be.

Smart contract origination fees can greatly exceed the total depositor base because the early liquidity in the first 50-100M USD worth of NAI, will create reference data for oracles to price derivatives and MakerDAO style spot liquidation, therefore, we could originate 1-2B in total NAI value in the first year and grow that with Circle or Tether like rates, the total addressable market growth ceiling is to encompass the domestic redemption demand of Nigeria, plus the financial demand for NAI as a funding currency, which may be even larger.
